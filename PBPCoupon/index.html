Pinebook Pro Coupon Redemption

If you are a community member who registered to the PINE64 Forum prior to July 1, 2019, you are eligible for pre-order priority on the Pinebook Pro and a free 128GB eMMC upgrade*.


*Note that the 128GB Pinebook Pro is limited in quantity to 1100 units. Once they are out of stock, you will receive a 64GB model instead.


After you enter your forum login, an email will be generated to you and to a member of the PINE64 Sales Team. At this point, you are guaranteed a coupon code.


Some members will receive their coupon within 72 hours, but the majority will need to wait to complete the process a couple of weeks (up-to a month). Please make sure to check your email spam folder for the confirmation email and, at a later date, the coupon code.
